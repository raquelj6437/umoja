Swipes for Boston

Date: November 10th
7 pm

Swipes for Boston is an extension of BU Student Government which run through its City Affairs Department. This dogged group of students comes together once a month to hand-deliver food, water, and other supplies to Boston’s homeless living on the streets.

Link: http://www.bu.edu/articles/2019/swipes-for-boston/
