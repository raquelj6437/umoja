Netflix Watch Party

Date: September 6th
7pm

The purpose of this program is to establish UMOJA’s consistency in having an event at the last Sunday of every month. Netflix Watch parties allow for a time to relax, support Netflix’s effort to include the Black sitcoms that many grew up with, and celebrate Black culture through film and TV. This event will provide space for the virtual and in-person to all watch shows together and enjoy each other’s reactions.
