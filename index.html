What's New

Umoja’s new and improved site now serves as the home for Boston University’s black community. This site features both on campus and off-campus resources that black organizations and our larger Umoja community can take advantage of and benefit from. In addition to this, our site includes an up to date calendar highlighting upcoming events hosted by Umoja and other black (or umbrella ) organizations on campus. This site allows for easy access to view Umoja’s social media platforms, executive board, history, and mission. With this new site, it has become easier to stay updated and connected with Umoja: The black student union.
